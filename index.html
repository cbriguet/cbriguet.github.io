Intro

I’m Product Manager for Security Analytics at VMware in the Security Business Unit. As a serial entrepreneur and Information Security specialist with two decades of successful experience, I led the design and product definition of world-class security solutions. I’m focused on User Behavior Analytics and Security Event Management.

My mantra is, “Good design can change the world,” and this mindset has allowed me to remove distractions and focus on clear, moonshot ideas.I co-founded my first security company almost 20 years ago in France. I moved to the Bay Area in 2008 for my second venture, which eventually was acquired by LogLogic / Tibco Software. In 2013 I joined The Hive, an incubator in Palo Alto to found E8 Security, a patented Machine-Learning driven security analytic solution designed to identify advanced persistent threats within enterprise networks.

In 2018, VMware acquired E8 Security to leverage behavior analytics for its secure digital workspace offering. My current research focuses on Security Analytics for Endpoint Detection and Response solutions.

Logcaliper App

The sizing tool for BIG data and SIEM!

Knowing the volume of data is critical for people that have to design a Big Data or Security Information and Event Management solution.

Since 2010, IT practitioners worldwide have been using Logcaliper to size and scope their Log Management / SIEM solution, convert various data sizes and rates units, and estimate storage capacity for long-term retention. Logcaliper is available for free on iPhones and iPads.

Conversion of message per second/hour/day/month/year and Bits/Bytes are done much faster and easier with Logcaliper.
